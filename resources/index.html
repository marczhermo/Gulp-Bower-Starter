For Sale

70 canopy hectares of Marlborough Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Noir, Pinot Gris, Chardonnay and Grunner at full production.

Here in Marlborough, New Zealand we are blessed with a special place in the world. A place where the terroir, climates of both day and night, unique landscapes and great people produce the world renowned sauvignon blanc and also the increasingly well known pinot noir and aromatic varieties.

This 70 canopy hectare vineyard is located in the Wairau Valley and we pride ourselves on having a fully sustainable vineyard of 70 hectares. Total production capacity is approximately 720 tonnes of Sauvignon Blanc, 80 tonnes of Pinot Noir, 60 tonnes of Pinot Gris along with Chardonnay and Grunner Veltliner blocks.

Suitable for overseas investors as we can assist with any Overseas Investment Office application.
